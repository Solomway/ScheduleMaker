Schedule Maker 0.1.1






Tutorial

To begin sign in or create an account in order to access your data, or click on continue as guest.

Then click on the 'Edit Employees' button to add, remove, and view your list of employees.

Once finished click on the 'Edit Shifts' button to add, remove, and view your list of shifts.

To create your schedule click on the 'Edit Schedule' button then select your starting date.

Next enter the number of days the schedule will be, and finally click on 'Generate Schedule'

***This website is in a unfinished state, and will continuously receive updates until it is usable to people of all businesses.***
